Zara
app driven
service based experience
second life & transparency
How do you keep customers engaged digitally whilst they’re already stood in your store?

Raising the bar when it comes to building a truly unified experience – Zara channel the online to offline journey into what we as consumers call ‘life’.

From targeted marketing & stock transparency to technological ‘self serve’ dressing rooms & pick up - Zara really are revolutionising the way that we shop in the masses. Helping customers to help themselves, freeing up employees to ensure stores are well stocked, and meet consumer values – with the likes of repair and resell options. 

And of course – connected every point in the customer journey to their digital profiles – truly a game changer for both the customer and the brand.
